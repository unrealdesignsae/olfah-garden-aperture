# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective homeowners and property investors comparing premium freehold residences in Sharjah. They need to understand Olfah's setting, homes, amenities, location, and ownership proposition quickly, then register interest or download the brochure with confidence.

## Product Purpose

Present Olfah by Alef Group as a distinctive forest-inspired residential community and turn visual interest into qualified enquiries. Success means visitors experience the scale and atmosphere of the place, find the complete project facts, and reach the enquiry path without friction.

## Brand Personality

Rooted, architectural, serene. The experience should feel confident and cultivated, with the warmth of late-afternoon landscape photography and the restraint of premium property editorial design.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic property portals, cold corporate blue, artificial neon, excessive glass panels, stock-photo polish, noisy sharpening, cross-hatched foliage, oversaturated greens, fake HDR, cluttered cards, and AI-generated changes to the supplied architectural scenes.

## Design Principles

1. Let the place lead: imagery carries the emotional argument and project facts remain easy to verify.
2. Preserve architectural truth: buildings, master-plan geometry, perspective, and camera framing stay anchored to the source while organic and atmospheric layers may be rebuilt for realism.
3. Create calm momentum: each section is a distinct chapter with deliberate pacing and purposeful motion.
4. Make premium feel useful: visual ambition must improve comprehension, navigation, and enquiry confidence.
5. Show complete evidence: every enhanced image remains available at a meaningful resolution and every project detail stays represented.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and keyboard behavior. Respect reduced-motion preferences, retain visible focus states, provide descriptive image alternatives, and keep interactions usable on touch, keyboard, and screen-reader navigation.
